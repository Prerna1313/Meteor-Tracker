Significant Meteor Events

Throughout Earth's history, meteor impacts have shaped our planet's evolution. Here are some of the most significant events recorded.

2.02 BILLION YEARS AGO

Vredefort Impact

The largest and one of the oldest confirmed impact structures on Earth, located in what is now South Africa. It has been highly eroded but is a testament to the colossal energies of early planetary impacts.

35 MILLION YEARS AGO

Popigai Impact

A massive impact in Siberia that created one of the largest craters on Earth. It is famous for creating billions of carats of "impact diamonds" from graphite in the rock at the time of the strike.

~8 km

Chesapeake Bay Impact

This impact created a massive underwater crater off the coast of Virginia. The effects of the impact, including a large earthquake and tsunamis, are still visible in the geological record today.
